Kourtney Kardashian is showing off her insane curves in new bathing suit photos and her fans are loving it. It’s hard to believe the 41-year-old is the mother of three children, as Kris Jenner’s eldest child is defying aging. Lounging around poolside in her Calabassas home, Kourtney wore a black bikini and showed off her toned figure and flawless abs. She rested her head against the crook of her arm and posed on her side. Kourtney has drawn plenty of attention for her figure along with her willingness to show off her flaws. Though at first glance, Kourtney looks perfect, she is the first to tell her fans that like all women,  she too has stretch marks.

Kourtney went viral when she shared several bathing suit photos where she showed off her stretch marks. Though you can’t see her marks in her newest bikini photo, she certainly became more relatable to many people once she showed off her imperfections.
