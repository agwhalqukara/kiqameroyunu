Cardi B Slams The Trolls Who Criticized Pregnant Yung Miami Following Her Scary Drive-By Shooting

After her car was shot at while she was at a recording studio in Miami, pregnant City Girls rapper, Yung Miami, received some hate on social media. Now, after the whole scary incident and the backlash, the outspoken Cardi B is defending the other woman.

Cardi slammed all of the trolls that judged Yung Miami during an Instagram Live session.

Here’s what she had to say!

As you can imagine, Cardi B is not at all happy about the hate her friend is getting following the very serious incident that took place in Miami a few days ago.

In case you did not know, Yung Miami is part of the female rap duo known as City Girls and according to multiple reports, as she was leaving Circle House Studios, her car was shot at!

Not too long after, a video that showed the rapper speaking with the police became viral and some people felt the need to drag her for supposedly ‘putting her unborn child in danger.’

Naturally, Cardi was very annoyed by the unnecessary criticism and took to IG Live to clap back for her friend.

‘On the comments joking about it. Doing little jokes… B***h shut the f**k up. This s**t is different. Y’all love to joke around. Just remember, those little comments that y’all be doing for clout that s**t is gonna be bad karma to you b***h, cuz that karma come. Y’all wanna be f***ing funny in the comments hoe, that shit come right back to you b***h,’ Cardi said while getting her makeup done.
