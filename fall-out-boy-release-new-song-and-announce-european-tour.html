Fall Out Boy Release New Song And Announce European Tour

September 09, 2024
blog

Fall Out Boy have dropped a brand new song, ‘Hold Me Tight or Don’t’, and announced a European tour.

‘Hold Me Tight or Don’t’ is the fourth single released from their upcoming album, Mania. The track follows on from ‘Young and Menace’, ‘Champion’ and ‘The Last of the Real Ones’. 

You can watch the music video for Fall Out Boy’s “Hold Me Tight or Don’t” below!

The band will be bringing the Mania tour across to the UK and mainland Europe in spring 2018. General sale tickets begin on Friday 24th November at 9 am GMT. To find out how to have access to pre-sale tickets, click here. Tour dates are as follows:

UK

27 March – Birmingham Arena, Birmingham 

28 March – Motorpoint Arena, Cardiff

29 March – Manchester Arena, Manchester

31 March – The O2, London

MAINLAND EUROPE

03 April – Zénith, Paris, France

04 April – AFAS Live, Amsterdam, Netherlands

06 April – Max-Schmelling-Halle, Berlin, Germany

07 April – Mehr! Theater, Hamburg, Germany

08 April – Zenith, Munich, Germany

10 April – Mitsubishi Electric Halle, Düsseldorf, Germany

12 April – Forest National, Brussels, Belgium

Fall Out Boy have teased that tour dates for Asia will be announced very soon, so be on the lookout! Mania is set to be released on the 19th January 2018. It’s available to pre-order here.
