Meghan King seems to have moved on after her divorce from President Joe Biden’s nephew, Cuffe Biden Owens. King and Owens married in October 2021, but their marriage barely lasted two months. Trevor Colhoun is King’s new partner, and they have been together since April 2022. They have not yet commented on their dating status. Following her divorce from Cuffe, King began dating Trevor, according to TMZ.

Everything there is to know about Meghan King’s new boyfriend

Trevor Colhoun is the Trusted Provider Network’s CEO and co-founder. It is a professional networking site designed to help licensed clinicians and approved treatment providers navigate the mental health sector. Colhoun joined the organization in 2018 with the goal of improving behavioral health outcomes via the use of technology and the prioritization of clinically matched treatment. It is also known as TPN.health and is one of the country’s biggest and fastest-growing behavioral networks. He has overseen restructurings and turnarounds of public and private enterprises such as Nexsan as a private equity investor and business leader.

He has served on a number of charity boards dedicated to the development of the nation’s young. In addition, he is a supporter of the Son of a Saint in New York. Trevor grew up in Connecticut and attended the University of Oregon. He lives in New Orleans and has extensive expertise in financial markets, corporate governance, and organizational leadership.

Meghan King’s personal life

Meghan King married Brad McDill in July 2007, and they separated in November 2011. In 2014, she married Jim Edmonds and became the stepmother to Edmonds’ four children. In November 2016, King and Edmonds welcomed their first child through IVF. They divorced in May 2021 after splitting in 2019. Meghan married Valerie Biden Owens’ son, Cuffe Biden Owens, an L.A.-based attorney, in an intimate wedding at Cuffe’s family home in October 2021. The couple divorced in December 2021, and Meghan revealed in 2022 that she intends to annul the marriage.

Meghan King began her career as a sales representative for a pathology software company. She rose to prominence after starring in the reality television program Resale Royalty. In 2014, her engagement to Jim Edmonds and quest for a wedding gown were highlighted on TLC’s Say Yes to the Dress. In 2015, Bravo announced that she will be joining the cast of The Real Housewives of Orange County for the tenth season as a wife. The next year, she returned for the 11th season.
