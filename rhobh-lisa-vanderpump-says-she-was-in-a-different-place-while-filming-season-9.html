RHOBH Lisa Vanderpump Says She Was In A Different Place While Filming Season 9

July 23, 2024
blog

Season 9 of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills promises to be one of the most drama-packed seasons to date. Not only is the entire cast returning for another round, but fans will also see cameos from Brandi Glanville and Denise Richards.

While fans are excited about what’s to come, RHOBH star Lisa Vanderpump revealed that she has not seen the trailer for the new season — and she has a good reason for skipping out on the fun.

“I haven’t seen the trailer. I won’t watch it because it was a very hard time in life this season,” Vanderpump shared.

Most of Vanderpump’s issues this season stem from the tragic passing of her brother, Mark Vanderpump. According to All About The Real Housewives, Vanderpump opened up about losing her sibling and admitted that she struggled hard after his death.

The Vanderpump Rules star confessed that, for the first time in her adult life, she was prescribed antidepressants and underwent counseling to help deal with her grief. But even with the meds, Vanderpump was extra emotional, and starring on the reality show didn’t help the situation.

While Vanderpump revealed that she shared a few laughs with her co-stars early on, she admitted that she was “in a different place” this season. As fans will recall, Vanderpump reportedly refused to film with her co-stars at one point, which stirred up a lot of drama outside the show.

This includes Vanderpump’s rumored feud with Lisa Rinna, with whom she suffered a major falling out this season.

Given everything Vanderpump went through last year, it is understandable that she had a difficult time in front of the cameras. We also get why she could not watch the Season 9 trailer, which probably would have opened up old wounds.

Hopefully things are going better for Lisa Vanderpump and she makes amends with all of her co-stars.
