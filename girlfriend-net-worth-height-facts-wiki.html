At the age of 22, William Byron has many NASCAR accolades. It is very surreal for the young racing prodigy to compete in every racing track to prove his career worth from sheer hard work and dedication.

American professional stock car racing driver William Byron currently competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series. He drives the No. 24 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 1LE for Hendrick Motorsports.

William Byron Bio & Family

The NASCAR driver was born as William McComas Byron Jr. on 29 November 1997 in Charlotte, North Carolina. Talking about his family, his parents, Bill and Dana Byron raised him along with a sister, named Kathryn Byron. He has a mother’s day tweet on 13 May 2018, which states,

Happy Mother’s Day to all the Moms out there and especially mine. Thanks for always supporting me!

William went to Charlotte Country Day School, also taking online classes through sponsor Liberty University, graduating in May 2016. Currently, he is a business communication student at Liberty University as per wiki. He stands at the height of 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 m) tall and weighs around 70 kg.

How Much Net Worth Does William Have?

The 22-year-old net worth glitters in million-dollar figures from his professional racing career since 2006. As a teenager, his racing began through iRacing simulator with more than 100 wins and 298 top fives in online competition. In 2012, he started his racing offline and, at age 15, began to transform as modern drivers and won 33 races. Two years later, he and JR Motorsports signed a program to continue Legends competition in 2012.

William then chose to sign a contract with NASCAR K&N Pro Series East in 2015. His succession driving for the NASCAR Xfinity Series started from 18 August 2016, and in 2017 he also won the NASCAR Xfinity Series Championship. Likewise, in 2018 he became the new replacement for Kasey Kahne in the No. 5 car, and the next year won the Rookie of the Year honors in 2019.
